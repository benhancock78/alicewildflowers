My Story


Hi, I'm Alice.

My passion for flowers started when I was just a small child and every summer our front garden would be covered in daisies, such a beautiful and inspirational sight! I loved picking the flowers and give them to my father.

My father would make wreaths at Christmas. I would sit watching, fascinated by how it came together to make something so beautiful, joining in as I got older.

I would collect different flowers then dry and press them between books to make a picture in a glass frame. My first job was at a local florist which I loved. It was such a magical shop and seems so romantic when I look back on it.

Having young children meant my dream took a back seat. But I started Alice & The Wildflowers having been to flower school and joined floral arranging classes. I have achieved the NAFAS Advanced Certificate in Floral Art & Design.

My passion is to provide fresh, seasonal flowers (where possible) to all events, whether weddings, corporate, funerals etc.

Arrangements can include sweet carts, vases, cake stand hoops, floral hoops or candelabras. My design style is both traditional and with a modern twist.

Thanks for visiting my site, I hope you like some of the work I've done.

Alice.
